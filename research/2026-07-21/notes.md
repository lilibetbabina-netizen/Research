## Findings

### 1. Inflationary nucleation via wineglass wormholes
- **Field:** physics
- **What changed:** Detailed study of axionic and magnetic wineglass wormholes shows that in the zero-charge limit the throat pinches off into a no-boundary instanton disconnected from asymptotic AdS, resolving puzzles such as negative wormhole actions.
- **Why it matters:** Offers a unified semiclassical picture of how expanding inflationary spacetimes can emerge and which instantons dominate probabilities.
- **Confidence:** medium
- **Key source:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11003

### 2. Topology-changing transitions in Euclidean gravity solutions
- **Field:** maths
- **What changed:** The same family of solutions exhibits a topology-changing transition between wormhole and no-boundary geometries as charge vanishes, with numerical solutions for scalar + axion/magnetic support.
- **Why it matters:** Makes precise a previously fuzzy relationship between two major proposals for cosmological initial conditions.
- **Confidence:** medium
- **Key source:** https://journals.aps.org/prd/accepted/10.1103/jpwp-2nzv
