## Top discoveries

### 1. SYK physics from shaken Hubbard lattices
Physical Review Letters work covered 23 Jul 2026 (Creffield et al.): periodically driving / “shaking” an optical lattice suppresses single-particle hopping and generates effective all-to-all-like interactions reproducing SYK hallmarks — strong chaos and fast information scrambling — on accessible cold-atom hardware.

**Why it matters:** Gives a realistic lab platform for black-hole-inspired quantum information dynamics and far-from-equilibrium many-body physics.
