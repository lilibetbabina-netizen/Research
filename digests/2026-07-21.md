# Physics & Maths Digest — 2026-07-21

Follow-through on semiclassical gravity: wineglass wormholes, no-boundary limits, and what small charge means for inflation length.

## Top discoveries

### 1. Inflationary nucleation via wineglass wormholes
Detailed study of axionic and magnetic wineglass wormholes shows that in the zero-charge limit the throat pinches off into a no-boundary instanton disconnected from asymptotic AdS, resolving puzzles such as negative wormhole actions.

**Why it matters:** Offers a unified semiclassical picture of how expanding inflationary spacetimes can emerge and which instantons dominate probabilities.

### 2. Topology-changing transitions in Euclidean gravity solutions
The same family of solutions exhibits a topology-changing transition between wormhole and no-boundary geometries as charge vanishes, with numerical solutions for scalar + axion/magnetic support.

**Why it matters:** Makes precise a previously fuzzy relationship between two major proposals for cosmological initial conditions.
